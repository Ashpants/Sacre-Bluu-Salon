Complete Starter Kit

Your deposit includes a comprehensive starter kit with all the professional tools and products you need to begin your training and build your career.

Get Your License

Our 300-hour program fulfills Utah state requirements for nail technician licensing. We prepare you for your licensing exams so you can get certified and start working.

Earn While You Learn

Unlike traditional programs, you'll earn income during your training by working with real clients in our salon. This hands-on approach means you graduate with both skills and practical experience.

Mentorship

We don't just teach you skills - we help you build your clientele, set up your business after graduation, and support you in a nurturing environment every step of the way.

Meet Your Instructor

Erin Jensen

Licensed Cosmetologist & Salon Owner

I've been a licensed professional since 2017, and in 2021 I made the switch to focus on nails full time, because I absolutely fell in love with the craft. When I first started out, I was frustrated by how unprepared I felt after school. I worked hard to close the gaps - taking extra classes, practicing constantly, and learning everything I could to truly master my skills.

That experience inspired me to create an apprenticeship program that makes the journey easier for others. My goal is to give aspiring nail techs the education, confidence, and real world experience I wish I'd had, so they can start their careers strong and build a business they're proud of.

Today, as a salon owner, I love mentoring my team, helping them grow their skills, their confidence, and their income. This program is my way of helping the next generation of nail artists do the same.
