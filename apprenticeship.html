Meet Your Instructor

Learn from experienced professionals

Erin Jensen

Licensed Cosmetologist & Salon Owner

Erin has been a licensed cosmetologist since 2017 and transitioned to nail specialization full-time in 2021. As the owner of Sacre Bluu Salon, she designed this apprenticeship program based on her own career journey to address the gaps she experienced in her initial training.

Her goal is to provide not just technical education, but the confidence and real-world experience that prepares graduates to start their careers strong and build a business they're proud of.

What You'll Learn

Comprehensive training in modern nail techniques

Structured Gel

Master the art of structured gel application, including full sets and fills for durable, beautiful nails.

Acrylic Techniques

Gel-X Application

Gel Polish & Manicures

Nail Art & Design

Client Management
