Salon Policies

Cancellation Policy

24 hours notice required for cancellations or rescheduling.

Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will incur a 50% service fee.

No-Show Policy

Clients who don't appear for scheduled appointments will be charged 100% of the service cost.

Payment & Booking

A credit card must be kept on file to reserve any service.

This is required for all appointments.

Children Policy

Please refrain from bringing children to the salon unless they are scheduled to receive services.
